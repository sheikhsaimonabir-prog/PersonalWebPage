Sandstorm Assault Helicopter

Overview

This project presents the conceptual design of an assault helicopter named “Sandstorm,” developed as a CAD modeling assignment for a Mechanical Engineering Drawing course. The design is inspired by the video game War Commander and focuses on translating a virtual concept into a detailed engineering model. Using CAD tools, the helicopter is modeled with key components such as the cockpit, rotor system, tail assembly, wings, landing gear, and weapon attachments, emphasizing structural layout and component integration. The project demonstrates fundamental skills in 3D modeling, part assembly, and technical drawing, exploration of aircraft design principles and engineering visualization.
